IP is owned by Ghost Galaxy, but here are my ideas on how to tie Keyforge's digital and physical experiences.

1. When you buy a pack digitally, it is added to your vault, so you may buy a paper copy of it.
2. Official stats card that shows event wins with that deck. This could also be implemented for in-person events, and the player could then buy a physical version.
3. Visual card cosmetics, like skins. If you buy a digital skin, you may buy the same skinned deck physically from the vault.
